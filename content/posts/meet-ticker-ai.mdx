## The Patterns Ticker AI Catches

Ticker AI is built to surface the behavioural patterns that quietly wreck retail accounts — the ones tied to **trading psychology**, not strategy. Over your trade history, it looks for things like:

- **Revenge trading** — jumping back in to "win it back" right after a loss
- **FOMO entries** — chasing a move that's already run
- **Overtrading** — too many trades on certain days, segments, or setups
- **Time-of-day weakness** — specific windows where your edge disappears
- **Tilt sizing** — increasing position size after wins or losses instead of staying consistent
- **Poor trade management** — cutting winners early, letting losers run
- **Expiry-day behaviour** — how your Nifty and Bank Nifty performance shifts on expiry

You don't have to ask for these one by one. Ticker AI flags the patterns it finds and, more importantly, tells you the concrete change to make next.
